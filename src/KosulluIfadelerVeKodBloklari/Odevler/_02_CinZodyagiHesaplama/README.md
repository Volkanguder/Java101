A Java program that takes the user's birth date and calculates their Chinese Zodiac 
sign.

What is the Chinese Zodiac?

The Chinese Zodiac is an astrology system that has been used for 4000 years in China. 
It categorizes people into 12 different zodiac signs or symbols. The Chinese Zodiac 
is a circular arrangement of these 12 signs, each representing an animal, with equal 
intervals of 10 degrees. It is not closely related to stars.

How is the Chinese Zodiac calculated?

To determine the Chinese Zodiac sign, the birth year is divided by 12, and the 
remainder is used.

Birth Year % 12 = 0 ➜ Monkey

Birth Year % 12 = 1 ➜ Rooster

Birth Year % 12 = 2 ➜ Dog

Birth Year % 12 = 3 ➜ Pig

Birth Year % 12 = 4 ➜ Rat

Birth Year % 12 = 5 ➜ Ox

Birth Year % 12 = 6 ➜ Tiger

Birth Year % 12 = 7 ➜ Rabbit

Birth Year % 12 = 8 ➜ Dragon

Birth Year % 12 = 9 ➜ Snake

Birth Year % 12 = 10 ➜ Horse

Birth Year % 12 = 11 ➜ Sheep
